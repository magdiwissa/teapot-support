Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add family members?

Go to Settings → Family & Partners → Add New Partner. Enter their email address and they'll receive an invitation to connect with you.

How do I share notes with specific family members?

When creating or editing a note, toggle on the family members you want to share with. Only selected members will see the note.

Why did my profile photo disappear?

This issue was fixed in v1.1! Make sure you're running the latest version. Profile photos now persist permanently across sign-ins.

How do I delete my account?

Go to Settings → scroll to bottom → Delete Account. This will permanently delete all your data including notes, tasks, and profile information.

Why am I not receiving notifications?

Check your iPhone Settings → Notifications → Teapot and make sure notifications are enabled. Also verify you have an internet connection.

How do I expand long notes?

Simply tap on the note's body text to expand it and see the full content. Tap again to collapse.

Where are my old notes?

The Notes screen shows only today's and upcoming notes. Past notes are filtered to keep your view clean and focused.

Is my data secure?

Yes! All data is encrypted in transit and stored securely using Google Firebase. Only you and family members you explicitly share with can see your content.
