Privacy Policy for Teapot

Last Updated: December 23, 2025

Introduction

Teapot ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application Teapot (the "App").

Please read this privacy policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this privacy policy, please do not access the application.

Information We Collect

Personal Information You Provide

When you use Teapot, we collect the following personal information:

  • Account Information: Email address, full name
  • Profile Information: Profile photo (optional), bio (optional)
  • User Content: Notes, tasks, events, and other content you create
  • Family Connections: Information about family members you connect with (names, email addresses)

Automatically Collected Information

When you use the App, we may automatically collect:

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version
  • Usage Data: App features used, interaction patterns
  • Authentication Data: Login timestamps, session information

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

1. Provide App Functionality

  • Create and manage your account
  • Store and sync your notes, tasks, and events
  • Enable family sharing features
  • Deliver push notifications for shared content

2. Improve the App

  • Analyze usage patterns to improve features
  • Fix bugs and technical issues
  • Develop new features

3. Communication

  • Send important updates about the App
  • Respond to your support requests
  • Notify you about shared content from family members

How We Share Your Information

With Family Members

When you use the family sharing features:

  • Notes, tasks, and events you choose to share are visible to selected family members
  • Your profile information (name, photo) is visible to connected family members
  • You control what content is shared with each family member

With Service Providers

We use the following third-party services to operate the App:

Google Firebase (provided by Google LLC)

  • Firebase Authentication: Manages user accounts and login
  • Cloud Firestore: Stores your notes, tasks, events, and profile data
  • Firebase Storage: Stores profile photos
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging: Delivers push notifications

Firebase Privacy Policy: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy

Apple Push Notification Service (provided by Apple Inc.)

  • Delivers notifications to your iOS device

Apple Privacy Policy: https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/

Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities (e.g., court orders, subpoenas).

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal information:

  • Encryption: Data is encrypted in transit using SSL/TLS
  • Firebase Security Rules: Database access is restricted to authenticated users
  • Authentication: Secure email/password and Google Sign-In
  • Access Control: You can only access your own data and shared content from family members

However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you services.

You can delete your account at any time through the App settings, which will:

  • Permanently delete your account information
  • Remove your profile data
  • Delete all your notes, tasks, and events
  • Remove you from family connections
  • Delete your profile photos from storage

Your Privacy Rights

You have the right to:

  1. Access: Request a copy of the personal information we hold about you
  2. Correction: Update or correct your personal information through the App
  3. Deletion: Delete your account and all associated data
  4. Data Portability: Request your data in a portable format
  5. Opt-Out: Disable push notifications through your device settings

How to Exercise Your Rights

  • Update Profile: Use Settings → Profile in the App
  • Delete Account: Use Settings → Delete Account in the App
  • Data Request: Contact us at info@magdiwissa.com

Children's Privacy

Teapot is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us, and we will delete such information.

California Privacy Rights

If you are a California resident, you have specific rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA):

  • Right to know what personal information is collected
  • Right to know whether personal information is sold or disclosed
  • Right to say no to the sale of personal information
  • Right to access your personal information
  • Right to request deletion of your personal information
  • Right to non-discrimination for exercising your rights

We do not sell your personal information.

European Privacy Rights (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):

  • Right of access
  • Right to rectification
  • Right to erasure
  • Right to restrict processing
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to object
  • Rights related to automated decision-making

Legal basis for processing: We process your data based on:

  • Your consent (when you create an account)
  • Contract performance (to provide the App services)
  • Legitimate interests (to improve the App)

International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and maintained on servers located outside of your state, province, country, or other governmental jurisdiction where data protection laws may differ.

We use Google Firebase, which has servers worldwide. Firebase complies with applicable data protection laws and provides appropriate safeguards for international data transfers.

Push Notifications

Teapot sends push notifications to alert you when:

  • Family members share notes, tasks, or events with you
  • You receive family connection requests

You can disable notifications at any time through:

  • iOS Settings → Notifications → Teapot
  • Or delete your account to stop all notifications

Third-Party Links

The App may contain links to third-party websites or services (e.g., Google Sign-In). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third parties. We encourage you to review their privacy policies.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy
  • Posting the new Privacy Policy in the App
  • Sending you a notification (for material changes)

Your continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Data Breach Notification

In the event of a data breach that affects your personal information, we will:

  • Notify you within 72 hours of becoming aware
  • Describe the nature of the breach
  • Explain the measures taken to address the breach
  • Provide recommendations to protect your information

Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us:

Email: info@magdiwissa.com
Support URL: https://magdiwissa.com/teapot-support

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority if you believe our processing of your personal information violates applicable law.
